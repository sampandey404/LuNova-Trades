🎯 Scanner Objective

  • Identify beaten-down large-cap stocks 15–60% below 52-week highs
  • Detect early recovery signals: MACD bullish crosses, RSI rising from oversold
  • Filter for institutional accumulation: long lower wicks at support
  • Confirm weak-trend environment: low ADX rules out strong downtrends
  • Score by technical confluence + social sentiment (Reddit, X, News)
  • Classify into recovery stages (Bottoming, Early, Confirmed)

🔍 The 6 Hard Filters

  • Filter 1: Drawdown 15–60% from ATH (avoids momentum names, momentum crashes)
  • Filter 2: Price > 200-week EMA (thesis break = elimination)
  • Filter 3: MACD histogram > 0 OR recent bullish cross
  • Filter 4: ADX < 32 OR falling (weak/dying trend allows entry)
  • Filter 5: RSI 28–54 (oversold recovery zone, no euphoria)
  • Filter 6: Lower wick ≥ 25% (rejects random bottoms, signals accumulation)

📈 Recovery Stages

  • Stage 1 (Bottoming): RSI < 38, price < EMA20 → Foundation forming
  • Stage 2 (Early): RSI 38–50, MACD+ → Nascent uptrend, tight ranges
  • Stage 3 (Confirmed): RSI > 50, price > EMA20, Stoch K > D → Trend validated

💾 Scoring (Max 13 points)

  • Technical (0–10): Stoch K>D (+2), RSI rising (+1), Price > EMA20 (+2), ADX < 20 (+1), Long wick > 50% (+1), MACD bullish (+2), ADX < 25 (+1)
  • Social Sentiment (0–3): X bullish > 50% (+1), News score > 0.2 (+1), X or News trend rising (+1)
  • Active Setup = tech ≥ 5 | Monitor = tech 3–4 | Discard = tech < 3
